# Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard** developed using Tableau. The objective of this dashboard is to analyze business performance across revenue, profit, products, and regions to support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor overall performance, identify profitable and loss-making products, and evaluate regional sales trends.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations need clear visibility into their sales and profit performance to make strategic decisions.

This dashboard answers key business questions:

- What is the overall sales and profit performance?
- Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
- Which sub-categories are generating losses?
- How are sales trending over time?
- Which regions perform best and worst?
- Are there products with high sales but low profitability?

---

### Visualizations Included

1. **Monthly Sales Trend** (Line Chart)  
   Displays seasonality and growth trends over time.

2. **Sales by Category** (Bar Chart)  
   Identifies top-performing categories.

3. **Profit by Sub-Category** (Bar Chart with Loss Highlight)  
   Highlights profitable and loss-making products.

4. **Sales by Region** (Geographic Map)  
   Shows regional sales distribution.

5. **Top 10 Products by Sales** (Bar Chart)  
   Identifies high-revenue products.

6. **Profit vs Sales Analysis** (Scatter Plot)  
   Detects products with high sales but low profit margins.

---

## Interactive Features

- Dynamic filters for Year, Category, Region, and Segment
- Cross-filtering between charts
- Highlighting and drill-down capabilities

---

## Key Insights Derived

- Certain sub-categories generate high revenue but low profitability.
- Sales show seasonal peaks during specific months.
- Some regions significantly outperform others in revenue generation.
- A small group of products contributes a large portion of total sales.

## Business Impact
